Technical Architecture

Modern, secure, and scalable stack across frontend, backend, and data layers.

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js for responsive UX. Backend: Node.js and Python, with AI for recommendations and matching. Database: SQL for institutional records, MongoDB for mentorship and achievements. Security: AES-256 at rest and SSL/TLS in transit. Verification: Government IDs and NAD/DigiLocker for credential authenticity.
